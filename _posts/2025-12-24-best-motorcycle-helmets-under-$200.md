**Section 1: Understanding Motorcycle Helmet Safety**
----------------------------------------------------

Before we dive into the best motorcycle helmets under $200, it's essential to understand what makes a helmet safe. Look for helmets that meet or exceed the safety standards set by the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) or the Snell Memorial Foundation. These organizations test helmets for impact resistance, penetration, and retention. Additionally, consider helmets with a face shield or visor to protect your eyes and face from the wind, bugs, and debris.

**Section 2: Types of Motorcycle Helmets**
--------------------------------------------

There are several types of motorcycle helmets available, each with its unique features and benefits. Here are a few examples:

* **Full-face helmets**: These helmets cover your entire head, including your face, and are ideal for track riding or commuting in heavy traffic.
* **Open-face helmets**: These helmets leave your face exposed, but offer excellent ventilation and visibility.
* **Half-helmets**: These helmets cover only the top of your head and are ideal for short trips or casual riding.
* **Modular helmets**: These helmets have a removable chin bar, allowing you to easily switch between full-face and open-face riding.
