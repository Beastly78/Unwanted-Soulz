**Protect Your Head with the Best Motorcycle Helmets Under $200**

Whether you're a seasoned biker or just starting out, wearing a motorcycle helmet is a crucial aspect of motorcycle safety. It can potentially save your life in the event of an accident and can even help reduce the severity of a crash.

However, not all helmets are created equal. You want a helmet that is both protective and comfortable to wear, without breaking the bank. In this article, we'll explore the best motorcycle helmets available for under $200.

**Section 1: What to Consider When Buying a Motorcycle Helmet**

Before we dive into our product recommendations, it's essential to consider a few things when buying a motorcycle helmet:

* **Safety standards**: Look for helmets that meet safety standards from organizations like the Department of Transportation (DOT) or the Snell Memorial Foundation.
* **Fit**: A helmet that fits properly is crucial for comfort and safety. Consider a helmet with an adjustable headband and chin strap.
* **Comfort**: A helmet with good ventilation and padding can make a big difference in your riding experience.
* **Style**: Consider a helmet that matches your personal style, but don't compromise on safety.

**Section 2: DOT vs. Snell Rated Helmets**

When it comes to motorcycle helmets, there are two main safety rating organizations: the Department of Transportation (DOT) and the Snell Memorial Foundation. Here's a brief overview of each:

* **DOT rated**: DOT rated helmets are required by law in many states and are designed to meet minimum safety standards.
* **Snell rated**: Snell rated helmets are considered to be safer and more protective than DOT rated helmets. They are designed to meet higher safety standards and are often used by professional riders.
