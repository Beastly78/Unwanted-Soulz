**## Section 1: Understanding Motorcycle Helmets**

Before we dive into the reviews, it's essential to understand what to look for in a motorcycle helmet. Here are some key factors to consider:

* **Snell Certification**: Look for a helmet certified by the Snell Memorial Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to setting safety standards for helmets.
* **Shell Size**: Ensure the helmet fits comfortably and securely. A smaller shell size can be a better option, as it provides a tighter fit and reduced noise levels.
* **Visor**: An adjustable visor can be a great feature, especially for riders who wear glasses or prefer a clear line of vision.
* **Comfort**: A comfortable helmet with breathable materials and a padded liner can make all the difference on long rides.
* **Weight**: A lighter helmet is generally easier to handle and reduce fatigue.

When shopping for a helmet under $200, it's essential to prioritize safety features. Here are some key factors to consider:
