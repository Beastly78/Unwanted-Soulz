**Budget Home Security Upgrades Anyone Can Do**
=====================================================

As a homeowner, security should always be at the top of your mind. Not only can it give you peace of mind, but it can also protect you and your loved ones from potential harm. However, investing in home security systems can be expensive and overwhelming. In this article, we will provide budget-friendly home security upgrades that anyone can do, ensuring your home is secure and protected without breaking the bank.

**1. Upgrade Your Door Locks**
=============================

One of the simplest and most effective upgrades you can make to your home security is upgrading your door locks. A deadbolt lock is a great addition to any home, but traditional deadbolt locks can be picked or broken with ease. To combat this, consider upgrading to smart door locks that use advanced technology, such as biometric identification, Bluetooth connectivity, and Wi-Fi capabilities.

### Smart Lock Recommendations:

#### **1. August Smart Lock (Pro)**

*   **Pros:**
    *   Compatible with Apple HomeKit and Google Assistant
    *   Uses Bluetooth and Wi-Fi for seamless connectivity
    *   Can be unlocked with a guest key
    *   Integrates with Amazon Alexa and Google Home
*   **Cons:**
    *   Requires a hub to function (additional cost)
    *   Can be expensive for larger homes
    *   Battery may need to be replaced frequently
*   **Cost:** Around $250

#### **2. Schlage Sense Smart Deadbolt**

*   **Pros:**
    *   Compatible with Amazon Alexa and Google Home
    *   Uses Bluetooth for seamless connectivity
    *   Has a backup key feature
    *   Offers keyless entry
*   **Cons:**
    *   Requires batteries that need to be replaced frequently
    *   Can be expensive for larger homes
    *   Does not integrate with Apple HomeKit
*   **Cost:** Around $230

#### **3. Ring Smart Door Lock**

*   **Pros:**
    *   Integrates with Amazon Alexa and Google Home

    *   Uses Wi-Fi for seamless connectivity
    *   Has a backup key feature

Upgrading your door locks with smart locks can significantly improve your home's security while still fitting within a budget.

**Conclusion**
==============

Budget-friendly home security upgrades are essential for every homeowner. By installing smart door locks, adding outdoor lighting, using security cameras, smart doorbells, and motion-activated sensors, you can significantly improve your home's security. Remember, investing in home security doesn't have to break the bank. With these budget-friendly upgrades, you can protect your home, family, and loved ones without compromising your financial stability.

**Final Tips:**
Make sure to research and compare the products before making a purchase. Many security systems require a subscription to use their full features, so be sure to factor that into your costs. Additionally, some security systems may require professional installation, so consider this cost when choosing your devices.
